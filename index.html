Air Fryer Mayhem

October 14, 2021 Jack Bruce

When I moved into my apartment this year, one of my roommates had just made a genius investment. He decided to buy an air fryer. With the air fryer we were able to make a whole bunch of different foods that would normally need a grill or take a super long time in the stove. But, with the air frier we could make those same kinds of foods but in a fraction of the time. It was great for the first few months; we were making a bunch of different really good foods. It even came with a little cookbook that we could try out some preplanned recipes. But, just this week, one of my other roommates went to make some pork chops and bam our frier was broken. For some reason it would cook for a minute exactly and then shut off, giving us an “E1” error message. We had no idea what that meant but it obviously wouldn’t work. So, now we’ve tried dissecting it to see if there was anything visibly wrong, which of course there wasn’t. Next on the to-do list is to call up the company to see if there’s any easy fix, but bottom line, we’re out an air frier.

Why Hollow Knight is a Masterpiece

August 23, 2020 Jack Bruce

Hollow Knight is a game made by Team Cherry in 2017 that absolutely changed the way I look at video games. In Hollow Knight you play as a nameless bug with a sword exploring a long forgotten ruined kingdom that has become plagued with a mysterious sickness. As you play you uncover more about the kingdom through the few characters that are still left and primarily through the world. Hollow Knight’s kingdom of Hallownest is a vast place with many different areas that have been crafted to tell the story of the kingdom not through dialogue but through the visuals to all those who take the time to look. In addition, Hollow Knight doesn’t hold your hand while you play. It’s brutally difficult at times and lets you go almost anywhere you want from the get-go, only barring your exploration for certain story beats. Exploration is at the forefront of Hollow Knight’s gameplay. There are a slew of different secrets that you can find, from power ups, special abilities, to even entire bosses that are left up to the player to find. Hollow Knight even put super secrets in various places around its world. Normal secrets are at least fairly noticeable to the player if they’re not ignoring everything but the path in front of them. But, these special secrets are not even visible to the player unless they are specifically looking in every nook and cranny to find. This level of craftsmanship and care that the developers put into Hollow Knight are why I believe it to be a masterpiece.
